Retrieve data from Spotify

Spotify keeps track of all your plays over the past year. Unfortunately it doesn't provide a decent way to
retrieve this data programmatically so you'll have to request this data manually.
  1. Login to spotify and go to the privacy page.
  2. Scroll to the very bottom and click on "Request".
  3. Prove you're not a robot and confirm the data request in your mail.
  4. Wait. This takes a maximum of 30 days, but mostly you'll receive an e-mail within a week.
  5. Download the zip and upload my_spotify_data.zip here.
